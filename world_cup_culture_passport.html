The World Cup of Culture

Same World Cup, different game. Instead of goals and penalty kicks, we're collecting passport stamps for the things that make each country unique: food, traditions, school life, animals, music and more.

Your mission: Visit every country below, read its page, and pick at least 5 facts per country to turn into trivia questions for your classmates. Look for the 🎯 Trivia Challenge box at the bottom of each page for inspiration — but the best questions are the ones you discover yourself!

📔 How this passport works

  1. Tap a flag stamp above to open that country's page.
  2. Read through the sections: Surprising Facts, Geography & Nature, School Life, Food, Traditions, and Teen Life.
  3. Jot down anything that surprises you — that's usually great trivia material!
  4. Use the Trivia Challenge box at the end of each page as a model for the questions you write.
  5. Try to collect all 10 stamps before class. Good luck, culture detective! 🕵️
